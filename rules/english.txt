1. 6 decks of cards. No hole card for dealer.

2. Minimum bet is 300, maximum bet is 10,000.
   Must be 100's multiple.

3. Dealers stand on soft 17 (Ex: A & 6).

4. Surrender returns 50% of placed bets.
   But, not allowed against dealer's Ace.

5. Blackjack (a combo of A & 10-valued card) pays 3 to 2,
   which is a profit 150% of placed bets.

6. A combo of A & 10-valued card after split counts as ordinary 21,
   not Blackjack.

7. Identically-valued combos (Ex: J & 10) can split 3 times to 4 hands
   at most, except for Aces, with just once per hand.

8. No hits allowed after Aces are split.

9. Double down on any 2 cards is allowed,
   except for Blackjack and split Aces.
